DESSERT

PISTACHIO STUFFED CHOCOLATE BAR

The viral Kanafeh-inspired Chocolate Bar from a dessert spot called The Fix in Dubai has been going viral and since lots of us are a plane ride away, we're going to make it at home and even better! Oozing with pistachio butter and crispy toasted shredded phyllo bits, this chocolate is the definition of indulgence. Every single bite is so creamy but also so crispy at the same time and the taste is so reminiscent of everyone's favorite Arab dessert!

PREP TIME: 1 HOUR

TOTAL TIME: 1 HOUR

YIELDS: 4 CHOCOLATE BARS




INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup pistachio paste/butter
    •  
  • 1 to 1/2 cup shredded phyllo or kataifi, cut into small pieces
    •  
  • vegetable oil, for frying
    •  
  • 2 to 3 cups milk chocolate, melted
    •  
  • 1/4 cup white chocolate, melted



INSTRUCTIONS

 
  1. Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan to 350Â°F.
    2.  
  2. Add the shredded phyllo in and fry until golden and crispy.
    3.  
  3. Set up a bowl with a strainer and pour the pan over to let the oil drain.
    4.  
  4. Allow the fried phyllo to cool down for 15 minutes.
    5.  
  5. Add the pistachio paste to a mixing bowl along with the fried phyllo and mix.
    6.  
  6. Add in about 1/4 cup or more of melted milk chocolate into chocolate mold.
    7.  
  7. Pop in the fridge or freezer for 10 to 15 minutes until hard.
    8.  
  8. Take the mold out and fill the chocolate mold with the pistachio and phyllo mixture.
    9.  
  9. Cover the filling entirely with more melted chocolate and pop it back in the fridge or freezer for 10 to 15 minutes until hard.
